# ADR-010: Legal Review Improvements

Status: REVIEW REQUIRED

## Core Position

Seven Days Derby shall be presented as a daily strategy game.

The economic system supports gameplay and long-term ecosystem
sustainability.

## Proposed Terminology

-   Buyback -\> Champion Program
-   Buyback Schedule -\> Champion Reward Schedule
-   Buyback Payment -\> Champion Reward
-   MLM -\> Support Network
-   MLM Reward -\> Community Support Reward (final naming TBD)

## Champion Program

Current implementation remains unchanged internally.

User-facing flow:

Day7 → Champion Horse → Champion Program → Champion Reward Schedule →
Memorial NFT

Only terminology and presentation change.

## NFT Position

Gameplay: Google Login → Wallet → Horse → Daily Derby

NFT remains implementation infrastructure.

Memorial NFT remains blockchain based.

## Marketplace

User-facing names:

Smart Marketplace Manual Marketplace

Manual listed horses are race-ineligible while listed.

## Support Network

Support allocation: - Triggered only by Burn. - Not generated merely by
referrals. - Based on gameplay. - Unclaimed allocation follows Community
Growth Pool policy.

## Community Growth Pool

Recommended:

40% Platform Reserve 40% Community Growth Pool 20% Emergency Reserve

## Marketing

Avoid emphasizing: - guaranteed earnings - investment - passive income

Emphasize: - Daily Derby - Stable - Training - Champion - Community -
Strategy

## Legal Review

Review before launch: - Japan - Malaysia - Target launch jurisdictions

Topics: - game regulation - referral regulation - crypto regulation -
advertising - marketplace structure

## Constitution

Seven Days Derby is a persistent daily strategy game.

Blockchain and settlement support gameplay rather than replace it.

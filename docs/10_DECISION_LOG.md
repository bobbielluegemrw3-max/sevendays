## Decisions 038-051 (2026-07-02, Owner)

038. Race formation v1.0: one race per day containing all ACTIVE horses. At large scale (e.g., 1,000,000 horses) the system may auto-split into multiple internal races for performance, but they are treated logically as one race with one global deterministic ranking.
039. Weather and Track Condition are derived deterministically from the committed `race_seed` via SHA-256 (Commit-Reveal). Weather is one of SUNNY, RAIN, CLOUDY, STORM. Track Condition uses the same SHA-256 derivation mechanism. AI, admin, and manual input are forbidden.
040. Condition and Fatigue use a daily deterministic recurrence: `condition_today = condition_yesterday + training_effect - fatigue`; `fatigue_today = fatigue_yesterday + training_cost - recovery`. Results clamp to the spec modifier ranges (condition_modifier -3.00..+3.00, fatigue_modifier -5.00..0.00). Exact numeric parameters (training_cost, recovery, initial values) are versioned in policy.
041. Valid direct referrer for MLM Reward = referrer with status ACTIVE only. BANNED, SUSPENDED, DELETED, self-referral, and referral loops are invalid; no MLM payment occurs.
042. Buyback payments are daily: due dates are D+1 through D+7 after Day7 Clear.
043. Purchase session lifecycle: cancellable until Marketplace Lock; locked sessions enter the batch; sessions unassigned in the batch are refunded and marked EXPIRED at batch completion. Purchase sessions do not persist across batches.
044. Economy metrics are deterministic formulas defined in economy_policy_version. v1.0: `p2p_match_rate = assigned P2P count / P2P listing count`; `rebuy_rate = burned owners repurchasing within 24h / burned owners`; `gmv_change_rate = (GMV_today - GMV_yesterday) / GMV_yesterday`. All remaining metrics (cash_coverage_ratio, buyback_liability_ratio, forecasted_cash_coverage) SHALL be formalized as deterministic formulas in the same style and submitted for owner approval before Phase 9 implementation.
045. Stress tests are deterministic parameterized scenarios. v1.0: Winter 30 = Mint demand -30%; Winter 90 = Mint demand -90%; Mass Withdrawal = 20% of all wallet balances withdrawn. Remaining scenarios (Base, High Survival, Low Burn, P2P Freeze, Buff Overpower) SHALL be parameterized in the same style and submitted for owner approval before Phase 9 implementation.
046. "AI" in this system means Deterministic Policy Engine (ordinary deterministic algorithms). LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any generative model) are forbidden in all production decision paths.
047. Daily batch time 20:00 is MYT (UTC+8). `batch_date` is defined in MYT.
048. Deposits use per-user HD wallet derived addresses. Shared deposit address is rejected.
049. Memorial NFT is minted on-chain as a permanent memorial. DB-only record is rejected. Chain and token standard default to Polygon PoS / ERC-721 pending final owner confirmation before Phase 7.
050. Horse names are generated by a deterministic Name Generator (not AI): Bloodline + Prefix + Suffix (e.g., Royal Thunder, Black Wind, Golden Storm), derived from mint_seed. Horse images are out of scope for v1.0 (future: Stable Diffusion / Flux generation).
051. A user may hold at most 10 concurrent purchase sessions (maximum lock 10 × 177.16 = 1,771.60 USDT).

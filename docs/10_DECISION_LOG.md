## Decisions 038-051 (2026-07-02, Owner)

038. Race formation v1.0: one race per day containing all ACTIVE horses. At large scale (e.g., 1,000,000 horses) the system may auto-split into multiple internal races for performance, but they are treated logically as one race with one global deterministic ranking.
039. Weather and Track Condition are derived deterministically from the committed `race_seed` via SHA-256 (Commit-Reveal). Weather is one of SUNNY, RAIN, CLOUDY, STORM. Track Condition uses the same SHA-256 derivation mechanism. AI, admin, and manual input are forbidden.
040. Condition and Fatigue use a daily deterministic recurrence: `condition_today = condition_yesterday + training_effect - fatigue`; `fatigue_today = fatigue_yesterday + training_cost - recovery`. Results clamp to the spec modifier ranges (condition_modifier -3.00..+3.00, fatigue_modifier -5.00..0.00). Exact numeric parameters (training_cost, recovery, initial values) are versioned in policy.
041. Valid direct referrer for MLM Reward = referrer with status ACTIVE only. BANNED, SUSPENDED, DELETED, self-referral, and referral loops are invalid; no MLM payment occurs.
042. Buyback payments are daily: due dates are D+1 through D+7 after Day7 Clear.
043. Purchase session lifecycle: cancellable until Marketplace Lock; locked sessions enter the batch; sessions unassigned in the batch are refunded and marked EXPIRED at batch completion. Purchase sessions do not persist across batches.
044. Economy metrics are deterministic formulas defined in economy_policy_version. v1.0: `p2p_match_rate = assigned P2P count / P2P listing count`; `rebuy_rate = burned owners repurchasing within 24h / burned owners`; `gmv_change_rate = (GMV_today - GMV_yesterday) / GMV_yesterday`. All remaining metrics (cash_coverage_ratio, buyback_liability_ratio, forecasted_cash_coverage) SHALL be formalized as deterministic formulas in the same style and submitted for owner approval before Phase 9 implementation.
045. Stress tests are deterministic parameterized scenarios. v1.0: Winter 30 = Mint demand -30%; Winter 90 = Mint demand -90%; Mass Withdrawal = 20% of all wallet balances withdrawn. Remaining scenarios (Base, High Survival, Low Burn, P2P Freeze, Buff Overpower) SHALL be parameterized in the same style and submitted for owner approval before Phase 9 implementation.
046. "AI" in this system means Deterministic Policy Engine (ordinary deterministic algorithms). LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any generative model) are forbidden in all production decision paths.
047. Daily batch time 20:00 is MYT (UTC+8). `batch_date` is defined in MYT.
048. Deposits use per-user HD wallet derived addresses. Shared deposit address is rejected.
049. Memorial NFT is minted on-chain as a permanent memorial. DB-only record is rejected. Chain and token standard default to Polygon PoS / ERC-721 pending final owner confirmation before Phase 7.
050. Horse names are generated by a deterministic Name Generator (not AI): Bloodline + Prefix + Suffix (e.g., Royal Thunder, Black Wind, Golden Storm), derived from mint_seed. Horse images are out of scope for v1.0 (future: Stable Diffusion / Flux generation).
051. A user may hold at most 10 concurrent purchase sessions (maximum lock 10 × 177.16 = 1,771.60 USDT).

## Decision 057 (2026-07-03, Owner)

057. Revenge Buff applies to exactly ONE race (resolves P9, option a). The buff auto-applies to the horse received in the user's next successful Assignment, is saved into that horse's next Race Participant Snapshot as the buff snapshot, adds +4/+7/+10 to that race's final_score, and becomes CONSUMED after that race regardless of Survive or Burn. Failed/refunded assignments do not consume it. Lifecycle: ACTIVE -> APPLIED (on assignment) -> snapshotted for the next race -> CONSUMED. Rationale: matches the Expected Buff Modifier +6.7 design; multi-race protection would inflate Day7 arrival rate and Buyback liability; keeps replay/audit simple.
Implementation note: CONSUMED is executed at snapshot inclusion (not after race completion). Snapshot inclusion is the irreversible commitment point — the immutable snapshot guarantees the buff affects exactly that one race whatever the outcome, so the two timings are financially and game-mechanically equivalent, and snapshot-time consumption is atomic with the snapshot itself.

## Decisions 058-059 (2026-07-03, Owner)

058. Economy metrics v1.0 (resolves P4). All metrics are calculated deterministically from ledger, buyback, marketplace, and batch records. LLM-based forecasting SHALL NOT be used in v1.0.
- Platform Liquid Reserves = PLATFORM_BUYBACK_RESERVE + PLATFORM_MLM_RESERVE + PLATFORM_EMERGENCY_RESERVE.
- cash_coverage_ratio = Platform Liquid Reserves / Next 30 Days Scheduled Buyback Payments (30-day window).
- buyback_cash_coverage_ratio = PLATFORM_BUYBACK_RESERVE / Next 30 Days Scheduled Buyback Payments (tracked separately for normal operations).
- buyback_liability_ratio = Total Unpaid Buyback Liability (all buyback_schedule_payments with status != PAID) / Platform Total Reserves (all four reserves). Lower is safer; >= 1.0 requires attention.
- forecasted_cash_coverage = Projected Liquid Reserves After 30 Days / Projected 30 Days Buyback Payments, where projected mint inflow uses avg_day0_mint_count_last_7d x 30 x reserve allocation amounts (deterministic projection input), minus projected MLM and operating usage; projected payments = scheduled next 30d + projected new buyback liability payments.

059. Stress test scenarios v1.0 (resolves P5). Daily runs, 30-day simulation horizon. Common pass conditions: cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00 every day, buyback_cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00 every day, no negative reserve balance, all due buyback payments payable.
- Base: current trend continues (7d averages for mints/match/rebuy, 30d averages for day7 arrival and burn). Pass: cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.20 for 30 days.
- High Survival: Day7 Arrival Rate +10 percentage points. Pass: buyback_cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00 for 30 days.
- Low Burn: burn count -30% (arrival rises accordingly). Pass: cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00.
- P2P Freeze: P2P demand = 0 for 7 days (match rate 0, only Day0 fallback if allowed, listings unassigned). Pass: all unassigned sessions refundable, no negative locked balance, buybacks payable, cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00.
- Buff Overpower: buff holders' effective survival impact +15 percentage points on projected arrivals (input: buff consumption avg last 7d). Pass: buyback_cash_coverage_ratio >= 1.00 for 30 days.
- Mass Withdrawal: 20% of all wallet balances withdrawn (Decision 045).
- Winter 30 / Winter 90: mint demand -30% / -90% (Decision 045).
Economy Status linkage on failure: Base fail -> at least WATCH; High Survival fail -> at least WINTER; P2P Freeze fail -> at least WATCH; Buff Overpower fail -> at least WINTER; any scenario with a buyback payment shortfall -> EMERGENCY. Stress results influence Tomorrow Economy Status per economy_policy_version.

## Decision 074 (2026-07-06, Owner)

074. Support Allowance (Burn Recovery Support) v2 — placement-based 7-TIER unilevel. Replaces the payout rule of Decision 021 (direct referrer, 10 USDT, one level); Decision 041's validity semantics carry over; the per-mint reserve allocation (5.40 to PLATFORM_MLM_RESERVE, Decision 024) is unchanged. Structure: TWO relations. (1) The immutable sponsor relation (referral link captured at signup; existing users.direct_referrer_user_id) drives unplaced-pool ownership and tier unlocking. (2) A placement tree is the payout path: referred users land in their sponsor's UNPLACED POOL and are hand-placed EXACTLY ONCE — directly under the sponsor (unlimited width) or under any node of the sponsor's own placement subtree; placement is immutable for everyone except an audited admin exception (UI must carry a strong "cannot be changed" warning); unplaced users can still refer and pre-arrange their own subtree, which attaches wholesale when they are placed; no placement deadline. Payout: on each burn, walk UP the placement tree at most 7 tiers and pay from PLATFORM_MLM_RESERVE — Tier1 3 USDT, Tier2 2 USDT, Tiers3-7 1 USDT each (total cap 10 USDT per burn, unchanged). An ancestor at distance d is paid only if (a) ACTIVE (an invalid tier is unclaimed — no compression/roll-up) and (b) the ancestor's unlocked tier count >= d. Tier unlocking is VOLUME-based, not headcount-based (sybil resistance: Google-auth multi-accounts are free, horses are not): the metric is the combined current value (PRICE_TABLE_V1) of ACTIVE horses held by the ancestor's DIRECT referrals (sponsor relation), evaluated deterministically at every 20:00 batch — tiers downgrade automatically the moment volume is not maintained. Thresholds (USDT): Tier1 from 0 (always unlocked), Tier2 >= 3,001, Tier3 >= 5,001, Tier4 >= 10,001, Tier5 >= 30,001, Tier6 >= 50,001, Tier7 >= 70,001 (fractional volumes between integer bounds fall to the lower tier). ALL unpaid amounts (locked/invalid tiers, chains shorter than 7, unplaced users, tree roots) remain in PLATFORM_MLM_RESERVE — the ADR's 40/40/20 redistribution is REJECTED (it would drain the reserve structurally under EMERGENCY burn rates; retained amounts build the operator's safety surplus). User-facing naming: "サポート手当 (Burn Recovery Support)" — never "commission" / "MLM reward" / "referral reward"; the stated consideration is supporting the burned player's recovery, funded by the pre-funded per-mint reserve (legal framing and lawyer-review memo: LEGAL_REVIEW_MEMO.md; marketing rules: PRELAUNCH_COPY_RISKS.md R3). Implementation spec: MLM_REVISION.md (phases M-1..M-5).

Amendment (2026-07-06, Owner): the user-facing name is サポートボーナス (Support Bonus), superseding "サポート手当". The product worldview is "your stable supported a fellow player's recovery, so a support bonus is paid" — the MLM concept is deliberately diluted everywhere in the product: never say MLM / commission / referral reward in any user-facing surface; the network page is presented as a support/invite network, not an organization chart.

"Buyback" was the one word in the product that read as an asset-redemption right and therefore as an investment product; the substance has always been a fixed, outcome-conditional achievement award (reach Day7 through seven daily races -> 200 USDT in seven installments + Memorial NFT), so the presentation is renamed to match the substance. User-facing terms: Champion Program / Champion Reward / Champion Horse; Japanese チャンピオン報酬 (statuses show チャンピオン). Page route /buybacks moves to /champion (old URLs redirect); the two BUYBACK_* notification templates are reworded (amends the Decision 065 verbatim texts); landing stats, horse/stable statuses, wallet labels, admin note and the Daily Derby show lines follow. NOTHING internal changes: DB tables (buyback_schedules...), ledger transaction types, API paths (/api/v1/buybacks) and notification type KEYS stay as-is — the rename is presentation-only and the legal memo continues to describe the substance honestly (LEGAL_REVIEW_MEMO adds a 景品表示法 review topic: Champion Reward as 景品類/懸賞 analysis, since the reward framing shifts the relevant lens from redemption to prize regulation). ADR-010's re-proposal of the 40/40/20 unclaimed-support redistribution is REJECTED again — Decision 074's full retention in PLATFORM_MLM_RESERVE stands (the monthly surplus sweep to a future Community Growth Pool remains a recorded option, revisited no earlier than the Item System work).
